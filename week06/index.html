Pharrell Williams and Nigo Team Up for Louis Vuitton Men’s Fall/Winter 2025 Show

Louis Vuitton presented the show for the Men's Fall-Winter 2025 Collection by Pharrell Williams and Nigo on Tuesday, January 21st in Paris.

Louis Vuitton's Fall/Winter 2025 Men's Collection, titled "Remember the Future," took on Paris for Men's Fashion Week, adding to the week's buzziest events and runway shows. Curated by Pharrell Williams and Nigo, the collection reflects their long-lasting friendship and influence on streetwear culture. Their debut brought a fresh perspective to Louis Vuitton’s signature craftsmanship, blending classic elements with contemporary aesthetics.

The collection paid homage to Nigo's vast archives of 20th-century workwear, reinterpreted through Pharrell's visionary stylistic approach. Earthy tones dominated the runway, with tailored silhouettes, oversized coats, and relaxed ensembles that reflect a balance between functionality and high fashion. Japanese motifs played a central role, and Louis Vuitton fused its signature monogram with traditional techniques like sashiko stitching and kasuri weaving.
